The Contractor XRay uses Augmented reality layovers to tell a builder or contractor about the wires, junction boxes or braces behind a wall. These information are to be loaded to a builders account based on his plans (Currently featuring how this data can be shown to a user). Currently added images of walls in the code although later this data can be provided directly fro the camera. Hence user can just photograph the wall and all the information is shown to him on the wall.

Key Functionality

  • Simultaneous detection and tracking of targetted wall
  • Load and activate multiple databases (Currently supporting 1.Cables and Junctions and 2.Braces)
  • Supports both front and rear camera (Would help once we get the overlay as transparent over the wall)
  • Manage camera functions: flash (during low light) and continuous autofocus (Single click does focus)

Instructions

  • Point camera at the Wall image to view
  • Single tap to focus
  • Double tap to access options menu
  • Select dataset for Cables and Junctions to see data on the Cables Wall and Junctions Wall above
  • Select dataset for Braces to see data on the Braces Wall and Junctions Wall above

I have used the opensource facilities provided by Vuforia to achieve this.
